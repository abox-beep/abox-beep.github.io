What does Lighthouse Map add to MCPE?
This place is created for those who want to test their survival skills in the wild Minecraft PE. The player has only a little island at his disposal, on which the lighthouse is located.




The map has a small territory, but there are many unusual details.

Resources
At first glance, it seems that there are no resources on the island, but this is not the case. If the user goes to the building, he will see chests there. They are located on each floor of the lighthouse.




By the way, some achievements are available on the map. For example, a Minecraft PE player can create an iron golem, since all the ingredients are available.

Interior
Inside the lighthouse, there are two floors, as well as two staircases. The interior of the map is quite interesting: there is everything for survival: a bed and other furniture, as well as six chests containing unusual items. By the way, there is one chest in the building that is different from all the others. Paintings are hanging on the walls, and the interior itself contains various decorative blocks.
