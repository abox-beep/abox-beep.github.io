Mining Simulator Map


Mining Simulator Map (1.20.1, 1.19.4) is a game map created by wchtig_. Are you good at mining? Try out this Minecraft Mining Simulator! In this fun game, your objective is to dig deep, extract valuable ores, sell them in the marketplace, and invest in upgrades. Explore different mining areas and uncover coal, iron, gold, diamonds, and more. Manage your resources wisely and choose between faster mining or expanding your storage capacity. Unlock new areas and discover rare Netherite ore. 

Become a mining tycoon and build your empire for the Win!
