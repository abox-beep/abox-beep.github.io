Volcano Island


This custom terrain features a small survival island situated somewhere far out in the ocean. On the island there's an active volcano with tons of lava. The terrain was most likely created using WorldPainter or a similar terraforming tool and then ported over to Bedrock Edition. It exists in a normal world terrain, so this means you can explore more than just the island in case you choose to use it for survival gameplay or similar. Creator: RyFol, Twitter Account, Website
