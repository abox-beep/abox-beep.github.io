3D Guns Mod


Stefinus 3D Guns Mod (1.7.10) adds in tons of awesome 3D guns. Featured in the mod are P90s, Glocks, RPGs, and more! Each weapon is also fully customizable with addon attachments and other goodies.

In this modâ€™s current state, it adds 11 guns, the P90 (SMG), the MP7 (smg), the Galil (Assault Rifle), ScarH (Assault Rifle), the M14 DMR (semi-auto sniper), the FRF2 (bolt action sniper rifle), the Glock 17 (pistol), the Minimi (LMG), M60 (LMG), the M1014 (semi automatic shotgun) and the Remington M47 (shotgun). There are various items added for you to be able to craft these guns aswell. For now there are only 3 attachements: the silencer and reflex/red dot sight and the holographic sight.

Features:
Shooting is bound to Attack/Destroy in the controls.
Aiming is bound to Use item/Place block in the controls.
Reloading is by default bound to R, but you can change it in the controls.
This is LAN/server compatible.
